Lasagne

    Ingredients:
  • 12 Pasta sheets
  • 500g Beef/Pork mince
  • 6 Slices of Prosciutto
  • 200ml Beef stock
  • 100g Mozzarella
  • 2 Beef tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 6 Garlic cloves
  • 70g White flour
  • 200g Butter
  • 200ml Milk
    Seasoning to one's taste:
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Nutmeg
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  1. Brown the mince then add onions, garlic, and prosciutto (salt, pepper, basil, oregano)
  2. Prepare bechamel sauce by adding butter and milk to a pan and gradually whisking in flour (nutmeg)
  3. Layer mince, bechamel, then 4 pasta sheets. Repeat to all in the dish
  4. Top with mozzarella
  5. Bake in oven for 45 mins at 200°C
